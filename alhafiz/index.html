Our Menu Highlights

Desi Flavours, Family Table

Al Hafiz Family Restaurant serves authentic Pakistani cuisine with a focus on flavour, freshness, and value. From hearty biryanis to rich daal dishes, our kitchen cooks with genuine ingredients and traditional recipes that satisfy every time you visit us at DHA, Lahore.

Biryani

Our biryani is prepared with aromatic basmati rice, tender meat, and a blend of fresh spices. Reviewed as quite good by Google customers, served with raita and salad for a complete satisfying desi meal experience.

Daal Mash & Lentils
